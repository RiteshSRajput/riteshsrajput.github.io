Connect, collaborate and share best practices

The Mastercard GRL Series offers a thought-provoking and informative forum to learn about the latest tools and techniques for minimizing fraud risk and maximizing profitability. Join us for the opportunity to connect, collaborate and share best practices with future partners and industry peers.

What Topics Are Covered?

  • Securing commerce by leveraging strong authentication
  • Intelligence and technology to address the latest cyber threats
  • The growing role of Artificial Intelligence in reducing fraud and risk
  • Interoperability and the importance of building and maintaining trust
  • Embracing disruption, challenge and change in the payments ecosystem
  • Impact of EMV-3DS on the fraud landscape
  • Biometrics and the growing importance of digital identity
  • Establishing high industry standards that benefit all
  • Developing trends in compliance, fraud and risk management

The GRLC Series is a platform that can help you:

  • Build your network and develop partnerships
  • Learn more about the smartest products and solutions to address fraud and risk in the payments ecosystem
  • Strategic thinking on how to manage risk through open discussions and pre and post conference workshops lead by experts
